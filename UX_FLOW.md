# UX Flow

## Primary demo journey

1. Recruiter opens Dashboard.
2. Recruiter sees that analysis is complete for all three pilot roles.
3. Recruiter selects DevOps Engineer as the default role.
4. Recruiter opens Smart Search and parses the JD.
5. Recruiter opens Market Mapping and reviews companies with this role.
6. Recruiter checks salary intelligence inside Market Mapping.
7. Recruiter opens Competitor Intel to understand demand pressure.
8. Recruiter opens Candidate Discovery and reviews ranked candidates.
9. Recruiter opens a candidate evidence breakdown.
10. Recruiter moves a candidate in Talent Pool.
11. Recruiter generates an Overview Report.

## UX principle

Every page should answer one recruiter question:

- Dashboard: What analyses are ready?
- Smart Search: What are we hiring for?
- Market Mapping: Where should we search?
- Competitor Intel: Who else is hiring?
- Candidate Discovery: Who should we approach first?
- Talent Pool: Who are we managing?
- Overview Report: What should we tell the hiring manager?
