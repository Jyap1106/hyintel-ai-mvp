# Demo Script

## Demo goal

Show that HyIntel AI helps a recruiter move from job description to market intelligence, competitor insight, candidate prioritization, talent pool management, and hiring-manager-ready reporting.

## Opening line

HyIntel AI is a compliance-first recruitment intelligence platform that helps Talent Acquisition teams make faster, explainable, human-reviewed hiring decisions using approved sample data.

## Walkthrough

### 1. Dashboard

Show that analysis has already been completed for:

- DevOps Engineer
- Finance Manager
- Customer Service Representative

Mention that the default role is DevOps Engineer in Malaysia FinTech.

### 2. Smart Search

Open the JD parser.  
Click Parse JD.  
Show must-have skills, nice-to-have skills, alternative titles, and Boolean strings.

### 3. Market Mapping

Show the hero table: All companies with this role.  
Point out employees in role, open roles, salary offered, experience range, hiring intensity, and sourcing recommendation.  
Mention that salary intelligence lives inside Market Mapping.

### 4. Competitor Intel

Show competitor demand and common skill requirements.

### 5. Candidate Discovery

Open Chen Wei Ming.  
Show 92% fit score.  
Explain that the score is transparent and based on six weighted evidence factors.

### 6. Talent Pool

Move a candidate from Reviewed to Shortlisted or HM Review.

### 7. Overview Report

Generate the market overview report.  
Show that it summarizes companies, open roles, salary benchmark, candidate availability, risks, and recommended next steps.

## Closing line

HyIntel AI does not replace recruiter judgment. It gives recruiters an explainable, compliant intelligence layer so they can brief hiring managers faster and with better evidence.
